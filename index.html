Nicolette Frangipane was born on September 29th, 1994, in Edison, New Jersey. Known professionally as Halsey, is an American singer, songwriter, and activist.

Halsey is noted for her distinctive singing voice. Outside of her career, she has been involved in suicide prevention awareness, sexual assault victim advocacy and racial justice protests.
